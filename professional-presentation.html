Electronic Commerce

Established in 2002, the major of e-commerce aims to produce high-level applied professionals who master the theoretical knowledge of management, know well about the information technology and information tools, and can operate computers proficiently. They know well about the management of current businesses, logistics and enterprises, and can use English fluently in work. They are able to undertaking jobs concerning technical operations, development and management of e-commerce, and e-government affairs in IT consulting firm, the information management department of multinationals or large enterprises, domestic financial institutions and government departments. The length of schooling is 4 years. Graduates will be granted bachelor’s degree in management, and their English ability can reach TEM 8 level.

E-commerce is an emerging major both domestically and internationally, which makes it promising in terms of its development and market, and which enables us to introduce the updated business and management concepts from abroad. This major closely follows the latest findings in scientific research and teaching so as to form a unique teaching pattern. Most teachers have obtained doctor’s degree from domestic or foreign universities, which makes them very capable in teaching and scientific research. Computer technology and the application of network technology are important to e-commerce, so advanced computer software and hardware are provided. This major produces student with innovative spirit.

Business Administration

Since September, 2009, the major of business administration has opened up pilot class of international business with all the major courses taught in English. This major aims to produce the high-level elites in international management. They are able to think global, participate in international cooperation and competition in English, and know well about the updated operation and management models of enterprises, so that they can work in multinationals operating in China, international financial institutions and the oversea businesses of Chinese enterprises.

IBS has offered the pilot class an excellent multi-disciplinary teaching team including professors, associate professors and lecturers. Most teachers have obtained both master’s degree in English and doctor’s degree in economics and management. They all have overseas study experience. Some courses will be taught by foreign professors.

During the recent teaching reform, we improved relevant courses with the focus on the cross-border and cross-cultural characteristics, and set a long-term goal to involve both domestic and foreign students in classes.

International Economics and Trade

As one of the earliest non-language majors of BFSU, the major of information management and information system was open for recruitment since the middle of 1980s. After the establishment of IBS, this major experienced adjustment in its teaching objectives based on market needs. More courses were opened in computer software application and information systems analysis and design, and it was not until 2002 that this major was officially established. The major is dedicated to produce elites with theoretical knowledge, knowledge in information science and technology, capability of proficiently operating computers, and knowledge and capability in information management and in information systems analysis and design.

All teachers of this major have obtained their doctor’s degree from domestic or oversea universities. They are capable in teaching and scientific research, and are able to incorporate the latest domestic or international achievement concerned in their teaching. Advanced business simulation laboratory and software for teaching information management were provided and introduced, which gives students a positive environment to strengthen their operative capability.

In the latest teaching reform, the major has shifted its focus to enhancing students’ basic technological skills, incorporated the latest findings in the teaching, and updated most of the courses. Meanwhile, practical operation was addressed so as to cultivate IT and entrepreneurial elites.

Accounting

In September, 2011, the major of accounting opened up pilot class of international finance management. Certain courses are taught in English. This major is arranged to produce the insightful elites who are able to deal with financial affairs of international businesses, know well about the risk, cost and value of enterprises, and formulate and implement business strategy from manager perspectives by fully utilizing the capital market. Graduates can undertake finance management jobs in large state-owned enterprises, multinationals operating in China, joint venture and international financial institutions. Also, after four years of study, students will be able to use English professionally and proficiently in work. Graduates will be granted a bachelor’s degree in management. Upon graduation, students’s English ability can reach TEM 8 level.

The pilot class is equipped with faculty consisting of professors, associate professors and lecturers. Most of the teachers have obtained their doctor’s degree in economics or management in famous domestic universities, and all of them have overseas study experience. Foreign professors will also be invited to teach courses.

In the major courses of accounting (international finance management), students will be using original English edition of teaching materials, and will be taught fully in English or English combined with Chinese. Also, foreign teaching pattern will be introduced.
