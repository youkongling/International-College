Exerci Tation Ullamcorper

Due to cultural background and different national conditions, higher education in developed countries such as Britain and the United States and our country's higher education have their own advantages in many aspects such as educational idea, knowledge constitution, teaching organization and teaching method. If we can make good use of the advantages of higher education in developed countries, and learn from their strengths, we will be able to cultivate a higher quality of talent for our socialist construction.

By adopting the "3 + 1" mode of cooperation, the Chinese and British sides will give full consideration to the national conditions of the two countries, complement each other's advantages in terms of the advantages of the two countries 'higher education, and make the students' comprehensive quality training the most convenient.
