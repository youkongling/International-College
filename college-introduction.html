  • College Introduction

International Business School was established in 2001, currently has nearly a thousand undergraduates in the school program. The college has six undergraduate majors: international economics and trade, finance, business administration (including international business direction), accounting (including international financial management direction), e-commerce, information management and information systems. After graduating from the first two professional bachelor's degree in economics, the latter four after graduation to grant a bachelor's degree in management (spare capacity students can choose to participate in double-degree courses in English, graduation in economics or management while taking a bachelor's degree , Won the Beijing Foreign Studies University double degree certificate issued by the English).

With the support of the Ministry of Education, Beijing Municipal Education Department and Beijing Foreign Studies University, after ten years of exploration and practice, the International Business School has formed a set of training mode of multinational economic management personnel Has made a series of remarkable achievements. In 2002, the School of Economics and Management of International Business School was identified as the key supporting discipline in the construction of the "211 Project" by Beijing Foreign Studies University. In 2007, the "Quality Project" of the Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Finance was approved as " Economic management personnel training experimental zone "; the same year, the economic management subject experimental teaching center was named" Beijing Experimental Teaching Demonstration Center "; in 2009, the college transnational economic management personnel training mode reform achievement was awarded" Beijing teaching achievement first class prize".

International Business School has a strong faculty, its areas of expertise in the economic, management, business English, IT and computer technology in all directions, and to a certain extent, mutual penetration and integration. College of full-time teachers, more than 90% have a Ph.D. or is studying for a doctoral degree, more than 70% have an overseas degree or more than six months overseas learning experience. College teachers each year a considerable number of papers published in the domestic and foreign well-known academic journals, hosted more than 10 national scientific research projects. College emphasizes the internationalization of teachers, a number of foreign professors in the hospital coaching, many teachers employed independent directors of listed companies or for enterprises engaged in management consulting work.

International Business School has established long-term cooperative relationship with many universities in the United States, Britain, Canada, Australia, Holland, Switzerland, Korea, India and other countries, and takes part in China's economic globalization in the most positive attitude. The college encourages undergraduate students to study abroad and provide them with opportunities for international exchange. At present, about 20 undergraduates go to overseas universities to exchange and study, but also some students to join the undergraduate classes, Chinese and foreign students to class exchange, not only enhance the cross-cultural communication skills, but also further promote the College The pace of internationalization.
