Wan Ming

secretary

Wan Ming, male, the Han nationality, born in March 1961, Jiangxi new people, in October 1984 to join the Communist Party of China, provincial Party school postgraduate education, professor, senior economist. Incumbent East China Jiaotong University Party committee secretary.

1978 September to 1982 July, studied at the Jiangxi University Department of philosophy, received a bachelor's degree.

From July 1982 to April 1987, he worked in the Automobile Transportation Corporation of Jiangxi Province and successively took the posts of Youth League Committee, Propaganda Department and Organization Department Cadres.

From April 1987 to January 2001, he worked as an inspection and collection bureau in the Communications Department of Jiangxi Province. He successively took the posts of Personnel Supervision Division Chief, Section Chief of Personnel Section, Chief of Section, Director of Xinyu Traffic Collection Branch, Director of Traffic Sub- 1995 September to 1998 July, to participate in Shandong University International Politics College of economic and legal professional postgraduate study;
